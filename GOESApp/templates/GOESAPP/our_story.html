Our Story

GOD ON EVERY SIDE - GOES: “For many who don't believe in God, life is guided by reason and experience; For us who do believe, it's shaped by faith and divine purpose”

Founded on Faith

Founded on the belief that God is with us in every step, every struggle, and every triumph, GOES represents the intersection of spirituality, street culture, and empowerment. Our designs reflect the diverse influences of music, art, and global trends, infused with messages of resilience, positivity, and purpose.

Designed with Purpose

Each piece in the GOES collection is designed for those who move through the world with conviction, who believe that style can reflect not just who you are, but who you are becoming. We create bold, intentional pieces for those who carry both style and substance. Every design reflects a simple truth: you are covered, guided, and empowered on every side.

Clothing with a Message

Join Something Bigger

At GOES, we create clothing with a message. Whether you’re rocking our latest drop or simply embodying your purpose, you are part of something bigger.
